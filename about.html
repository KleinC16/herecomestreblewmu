About Us

Here Comes Treble is an Acapella group at Western Michigan University focused on growth and friendship. With members that share a strong passion for music, we are dedicated to bringing our audience the most moving experience possible. We sing for the love of singing, and showcase all of our original members through compromise and flexibility.

Feel free to contact us if you have any inquiries!
